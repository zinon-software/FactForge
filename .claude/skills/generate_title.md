# Skill: Generate Title
# Use when creating YouTube titles for any video

---

## The Core Formula

**[Shocking fact/number] + [Emotional trigger] + [Curiosity gap]**

The title must:
1. Create an immediate "wait, what?" reaction
2. Promise something surprising the viewer doesn't know
3. Feel like the viewer will be embarrassed NOT to know this
4. Be under 60 characters for mobile truncation safety

---

## Power Words (use at least one)

**Shock:** Banned, Secret, Hidden, Real, Actual, Exposed, Revealed
**Scale:** Richer Than, More Than, Times Bigger, Larger Than
**Contrast:** vs, vs What You Think, While You, But Nobody Talks About
**Curiosity:** Why, How, The Reason, The Truth, What Happened When

---

## Proven Title Formulas (use these as templates)

1. **Number + Superlative + Topic**
   "The Man Who Was Richer Than All US Presidents Combined"

2. **Country A vs Country B + Surprising Claim**
   "China Built More Skyscrapers in 2 Years Than the US Did in 50"

3. **Shocking Number + Context**
   "One King Owned 25 Percent of All the World's Wealth"

4. **Myth vs Reality**
   "The US Military Budget Is Larger Than the Next 10 Countries Combined"

5. **Hidden Fact**
   "The Medieval City That Was Larger Than Paris and London Combined"

6. **Price Reveal**
   "Why One Kilogram of This Common Material Costs 3 Million Dollars"

7. **Timeline Shock**
   "The Roman Empire Was Already 1000 Years Old When Islam Was Founded"

8. **Comparative Scale**
   "If Jeff Bezos Spent One Million Dollars Per Day He Would Die Broke in 400 Years"

---

## Title Scoring System

Generate 5 title variants for every video. Score each on:

| Criterion | Points |
|-----------|--------|
| Contains a specific number | +20 |
| Contains a power word | +15 |
| Creates clear curiosity gap | +25 |
| Under 60 characters | +10 |
| Triggers debate or disbelief | +20 |
| Matches a proven formula | +10 |

**Maximum score: 100. Select the highest scoring title.**

---

## Weak Title Examples (never write these)

❌ "Interesting Facts About the Ottoman Empire"
❌ "You Won't Believe This!"
❌ "Amazing Historical Facts #47"
❌ "Things You Didn't Know"
❌ "The Truth About History" (too vague)

---

## Strong Title Examples (write these instead)

✅ "The Ottoman Empire Was Larger Than the Entire United States"
✅ "Mansa Musa Was So Rich His Charity Caused a Decade-Long Economic Depression"
✅ "The Arab World Had Universities 500 Years Before Oxford Existed"
✅ "China's Military Spending Doubled in the Time It Takes to Read This Title"
✅ "This African Empire Controlled More Gold Than All of Europe Combined"

---

## Forbidden Words (YouTube penalizes these)

- "shocking" in caps (SHOCKING) — spam signal
- Excessive punctuation (!!!) — spam signal
- All caps titles — spam signal
- Misleading claims (anything you can't prove in the video)
- Sensationalized tragedy framing

---

## Multi-Language Title Notes

When generating translated titles:
- Translate the MEANING, not word-for-word
- Power words must be equivalent in target language
- Numbers stay as numerals (more universal)
- Arabic titles: use Modern Standard Arabic (MSA) for broader reach
